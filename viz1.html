Northern Hemisphere: Max Temperature vs. Latitude

Northern hemisphere data was pulled from the cities.csv using a loc for latitude coordinates greater than 0 degrees in Pandas. The scipy.stats module was imported to perform a linear regression max temp vs. latitude and r was calculated. The r-value was found to be: 3741206817424309. The line equation can be found in the image below. Since the r value is larger than .7, there is a strong positive relationship between maximum temperature and latitude in cities located in the northern hemisphere.
