Northern Hemisphere:
Max Temperature vs. Latitude

Northern hemisphere data was pulled from the cities.csv using a loc for latitude coordinates greater than 0 degrees in Pandas. The scipy.stats module was imported to perform a linear regression max temp vs. latitude and r was calculated. The r-value was found to be: -0.8594328572452402. The line equation can be found in the image below. Since the r value is larger than .7, there is a strong negative relationship between maximum temperature and latitude in cities located in the northern hemisphere. This is to be expected since cities near the equator (latitude=0) have higher temperatures.
